Method 2: Use the Files App (View Only)

If you just want to view a password-protected PDF without permanently removing the password, the built-in Files app on iPhone can help:

  1. Open the Files app on your iPhone
  2. Find and tap the password-protected PDF
  3. Enter the password when prompted
  4. The PDF opens in the built-in viewer

This lets you view the PDF but doesn't remove the password permanently. Every time you open the file, you'll still need to enter the password.

Method 3: Use the Books App

Apple's Books app also supports password-protected PDFs:

  1. Find the PDF in Files or Mail
  2. Tap the share icon → Copy to Books
  3. Open Books and tap the PDF
  4. Enter the password

Again, this lets you read it but doesn't strip the password. For permanent removal, use Method 1.

Method 4: Use a Shortcut (Advanced)
